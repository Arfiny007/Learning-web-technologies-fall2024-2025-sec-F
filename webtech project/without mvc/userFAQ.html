Tendify Online Shop - FAQ

1. What is Tendify?

Tendify is an online shop where you can find a wide range of products, from clothing to electronics, delivered right to your door.

2. How do I create an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage, fill in your details, and start shopping!

3. What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit/debit cards, mobile payments, and cash on delivery.

4. How can I track my order?

Once your order is placed, you will receive a tracking link via email or SMS to monitor your delivery status.

5. Can I return a product?

Yes, products can be returned within 7 days of delivery if they meet our return policy.

6. How do I cancel my order?

You can cancel your order before it is shipped by visiting the "My Orders" section or contacting our support team.
